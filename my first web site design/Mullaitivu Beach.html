Mullaitivu Beach

Beautiful beach place. but this place is not suitable for bathing . Infront of the beach there is a children's Park . Actually this is like small Harbor . It is because Sri Lankan small boat fishermen brought their fish here and sell. There are fish markets also. Good for visitors early in the morning or afternoon to visit. Be aware of cows .

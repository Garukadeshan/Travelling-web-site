Bopitiya Beach

Beautiful to spend a morning jogging or an evening walk with your loving ones. Less crowd normally. There is no any shop to buy anything around the beach but you can find them from the main street with more Hotels by side the beach. You can get a best view of Colombo City and the Sea with good wind and having a nice day end before dinner. Keep clean

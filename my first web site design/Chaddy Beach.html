Chaddy Beach
Chaddy Beach

This is a beach situated in the southern part of the Jaffna peninsula and is not very popular as Casuarina Beach. It is a safe beach to have a bath and not very crowded. Has additional facilities such as fresh water showers, changing rooms and wash rooms. Best time to visit is either early in the morning or late evening.
